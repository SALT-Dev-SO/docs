## Board of Directors

Board governance is managed through the D&O Dashboard, which provides:

- **Board meeting materials** — Presentations and financial reports
- **Cap table management** — Equity ownership tracking with Carta integration
- **Options grants** — Interactive grant modeling with scenario comparison
- **Treasury oversight** — Real-time Bitcoin reserve monitoring
