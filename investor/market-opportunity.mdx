## Market Overview

SALT Lending operates in the digital asset lending market, providing crypto-backed loans to both retail and institutional borrowers. The market opportunity spans multiple segments including personal lending, corporate lending, enterprise solutions, and jurisdictional expansion.

## Competitive Landscape

SALT differentiates through:

- **Regulatory-first approach** — Operating within compliance frameworks across jurisdictions
- **Multi-collateral support** — Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other digital assets as collateral
- **Institutional grade** — Enterprise lending products for corporate treasuries
- **Technology platform** — Proprietary lending infrastructure with real-time risk management
