## Partnership Ecosystem

SALT's partnership strategy focuses on three areas:

1. **Technology Partners** — Infrastructure and platform integrations
2. **Distribution Partners** — Channel partnerships for loan origination
3. **Custody & Security** — Digital asset custody and insurance providers

## Key Integrations

| Partner Type | Function | Status |
|-------------|----------|--------|
| Custody | Digital asset safekeeping | Active |
| Banking | Fiat rails and settlement | Active |
| Compliance | KYC/AML verification | Active |
| Insurance | Collateral protection | Active |
