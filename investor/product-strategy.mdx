## Lending Products

SALT offers crypto-backed lending across multiple tiers:

| Product | Borrower Type | Collateral | Key Features |
|---------|--------------|------------|-------------|
| Personal Loans | Retail | BTC, ETH | Flexible terms, no credit check |
| Corporate Lending | Business | BTC, ETH, multi-asset | Higher limits, custom terms |
| Enterprise Solutions | Institutional | Multi-collateral | White-label, API access |
| Jurisdictional Expansion | Cross-border | Region-specific | Regulatory compliance per jurisdiction |

## Technology Platform

The lending platform consists of several integrated systems:

- **Loan Origination** — Automated underwriting and collateral management
- **Risk Engine** — Real-time LTV monitoring and margin call automation
- **Treasury Management** — Bitcoin reserve tracking and allocation
- **API Infrastructure** — RESTful APIs for partner integration

## Growth Strategy

SALT's expansion follows a phased approach:

1. **Phase 1** — Core personal and corporate lending (current)
2. **Phase 2** — Enterprise and institutional products
3. **Phase 3** — Jurisdictional expansion into new markets
4. **Phase 4** — Platform-as-a-service for partner lending programs
