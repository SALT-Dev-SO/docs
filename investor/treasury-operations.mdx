## Bitcoin Treasury Strategy

SALT maintains a Bitcoin treasury position as part of its balance sheet strategy, leveraging its core lending expertise for reserve management.

## Treasury Allocation

The treasury model covers:

- **De-SPAC Cash** — Transaction proceeds allocation
- **BTC Reserve** — Bitcoin holdings for platform liquidity
- **Working Capital** — Operational runway and growth investment
- **Treasury Revenue Model** — Yield generation from reserve assets
